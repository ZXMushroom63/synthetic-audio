Privacy Policy

Questions or concerns? Email zxmushroom63 [at] gmail (dot) com

The SYNTHETIC Audio web app & multiplayer server collects no personal information. The user's IP Address may be temporarily stored during multiplayer sessions, but is removed once the user disconnects, and is not accessible to other users. When accessing the service through Discord, the user's IP is never stored at all.

User generated content is stored fully offline, in the browser's localStorage and IndexedDB databases. When using multiplayer services, user generated content will be stored temporarily on the server, for up to 30 minutes after the last user leaves the multiplayer session.

If you have reason to believe we may have mishandled any potential personal information, contact us at the email at the start of this page for a response & agreement.
